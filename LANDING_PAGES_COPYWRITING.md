## COPYWRITING PRINCIPLES

### The TradersYard Voice
- **Confident but not arrogant**: We know we're good, but we let the numbers speak
- **Direct and honest**: No fluff, no hype, just facts
- **Trader-to-trader**: We speak their language (drawdown, profit split, lot sizes)
- **Empathetic**: We understand their fears (scams, losing money, failing)

### Power Words to Use
```
Funded | Capital | Profit | Payout | Instant | Proven | Real
Keep | Earn | Trade | Pass | Scale | Withdraw | Your
```

### Words to Avoid
```
Easy | Guaranteed | Risk-free | Secret | Trick | Hack | Quick
Get rich | Passive income | No experience needed
```
